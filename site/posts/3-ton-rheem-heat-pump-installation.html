3-Ton Rheem Heat Pump Installation

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Chino Hills, CA

Project Overview

BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating recently completed a 3-Ton Rheem Heat Pump installation at a residence in the Southern California area. The existing system had reached the end of its reliable service life, prompting the homeowner to seek a modern replacement focused on consistent comfort and lower operating costs.

Why Choose a 3-Ton Rheem Heat Pump

A 3-ton capacity matches the cooling and heating load of many single-family homes in the region. Rheem units are selected for their durable construction, strong warranty coverage, and efficient performance across varying temperatures. The heat pump provides both cooling in summer and heating in winter from a single piece of equipment, reducing the need for separate systems.

Installation Process

The project began with removal of the old equipment and careful inspection of the existing ductwork and electrical connections. New line sets were installed to maintain proper refrigerant flow, and the outdoor unit was positioned on a level pad with adequate clearance for airflow. Inside, the air handler was set in place and connected, followed by system charging, electrical wiring, and a full operational test. All work followed manufacturer guidelines and local codes.

Results and Homeowner Benefits

The new system delivers steady temperatures throughout the home with noticeably quieter operation. Homeowners gain improved energy efficiency, reliable year-round performance, and reduced maintenance needs compared with the previous aging equipment. Proper sizing and professional installation help maximize comfort while keeping utility costs in check.
