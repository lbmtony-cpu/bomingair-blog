5-Ton American Standard AC System Installation

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Southern California

Project Overview

BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating recently completed a 5-Ton American Standard AC System Installation at a home in the Southern California area. The existing equipment had reached the end of its reliable service life, leading to inconsistent cooling and higher energy use during peak summer months.

Why a 5-Ton American Standard Unit Was Selected

A 5-ton capacity matched the home's cooling load calculations. American Standard equipment is known for durable compressors, corrosion-resistant coils, and strong warranty coverage. These features support dependable performance in the hot, dry conditions common across the Chino Hills area and surrounding communities.

Installation Process

The team removed the old condensing unit and indoor coil, then set the new American Standard outdoor unit on a level pad with proper clearances. New refrigerant lines were run and pressure-tested, the evaporator coil was installed in the existing air handler, and all electrical connections were updated to code. The system was charged, commissioned, and tested for airflow and temperature differential before handover.

Results and Homeowner Benefits

The new system provides even cooling throughout the house with noticeably quieter operation. Homeowners can expect improved energy efficiency compared with the previous aging unit, along with reliable comfort during extended heat waves. Regular maintenance will help preserve performance and protect the manufacturer's warranty.
