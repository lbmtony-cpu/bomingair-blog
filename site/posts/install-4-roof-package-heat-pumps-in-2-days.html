Install 4 Roof Package Heat Pumps in 2 Days

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Southern California

Project Scope

A commercial property in the Southern California area needed a complete upgrade of its aging rooftop HVAC systems. The solution was to install 4 roof package heat pumps in 2 days, replacing outdated units with modern, all-in-one heating and cooling equipment designed for flat-roof applications.

Why Roof Package Heat Pumps

Roof package units combine the condenser, evaporator, and heating components in a single cabinet. This design reduces ductwork requirements and frees up ground space. The new units offer improved energy efficiency, quieter operation, and better temperature control compared with older models, making them well-suited to the region’s warm summers and mild winters.

Two-Day Installation Process

The BOMING team began by safely removing the existing equipment and preparing the roof curbs. New package units were lifted into place using proper rigging methods. Electrical connections, gas lines, and condensate drains were completed, followed by thorough testing of each system. Because the work was finished over two consecutive days, business operations experienced minimal disruption.

Results and Long-Term Value

The property now benefits from consistent comfort across all zones, lower operating costs, and reduced risk of unexpected breakdowns. Regular maintenance from a licensed Chino Hills contractor will help the systems reach their expected service life. Property owners seeking similar rooftop HVAC upgrades can contact BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating to discuss options for their buildings.

How long does a typical roof package heat pump installation take?

Are roof package units suitable for Southern California commercial buildings?

Yes. Their compact design and combined heating-cooling functions perform well in the local climate while conserving ground space.

What maintenance do new package heat pumps require?

Annual professional inspections, filter changes, and coil cleaning help maintain efficiency and reliability.
