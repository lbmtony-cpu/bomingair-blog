1.5 Ton TCL Mini-Split System Installation

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Norco, CA

Project Overview

A property owner in the Southern California area contacted BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating to address inconsistent temperatures in a single room that lacked existing ductwork. After evaluating the space and cooling load, the team recommended and installed a 1.5 Ton TCL Mini-Split system to provide targeted, efficient climate control without major renovations.

Why a 1.5 Ton TCL Mini-Split System

The 1.5 Ton TCL Mini-Split system offers inverter-driven technology that adjusts output to match the room’s needs, reducing energy waste compared with older window units or oversized central systems. Its compact indoor head mounts high on the wall while the outdoor condenser remains discreet, making it suitable for homes and small businesses where space and aesthetics matter. TCL units also carry strong warranty coverage and reliable performance in the region’s variable climate.

Installation Process

Technicians began by mounting the indoor evaporator unit and running refrigerant and condensate lines through a small wall penetration to the outdoor location. Electrical connections were made to a dedicated circuit, and the line set was vacuumed and tested for leaks before charging the system. Final steps included programming the wireless remote, verifying airflow, and walking the customer through basic operation and filter maintenance. The entire process was completed in one day with minimal disruption to the interior.

Results and Ongoing Benefits

The new 1.5 Ton TCL Mini-Split system now delivers steady, quiet cooling exactly where it is needed. Homeowners gain independent temperature control for that space, lower monthly energy use, and the peace of mind that comes with professional installation and support from a licensed local contractor. Regular filter cleaning and annual professional service will keep the equipment running efficiently for years.

Frequently asked questions

How long does a 1.5 Ton TCL Mini-Split system installation usually take?

Most single-zone installations are completed in one day, including mounting, line-set routing, electrical work, and system testing.

Is a mini-split system a good choice for rooms without ducts?

Yes. A ductless mini-split provides efficient, zoned cooling or heating without the need to install or modify ductwork.

What maintenance does the TCL unit require?

Homeowners should clean or replace filters monthly during heavy use and schedule annual professional service to check refrigerant levels and electrical components.
