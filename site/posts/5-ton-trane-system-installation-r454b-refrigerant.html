5 Ton Trane System Installation with R454B Refrigerant

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Southern California

Project Overview

BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating recently completed an Installation of 5 Ton Trane System , R454b new refrigerant at a residence in the Southern California area. The existing equipment had reached the end of its reliable service life, prompting the homeowner to seek a modern, efficient replacement that meets current environmental standards.

Equipment Selection and Benefits

The 5 Ton Trane system paired with R454B refrigerant was chosen for its strong performance in larger homes and its lower global warming potential compared with older refrigerants. Trane units are known for durable compressors and consistent airflow, while R454B supports efficient heat transfer and future regulatory compliance. Homeowners gain improved temperature control, quieter operation, and potential reductions in long-term energy use without sacrificing comfort during hot summers or mild winters.

Installation Process

Technicians began by safely recovering the old refrigerant and removing the previous indoor and outdoor units. New line sets were installed where needed, followed by placement of the Trane air handler and condensing unit. All electrical connections, condensate drains, and thermostat wiring were updated to manufacturer specifications. The team performed a full system evacuation, charged the unit with R454B, and conducted multiple test cycles to verify proper pressures and airflow before final commissioning.

Results for the Homeowner

The new system now delivers steady, even cooling throughout the property with noticeably lower sound levels. Because R454B is a mildly flammable A2L refrigerant, all safety sensors and installation procedures followed current best practices. What makes R454B different from older refrigerants?

R454B offers a significantly lower global warming potential while maintaining strong cooling performance, making it a forward-looking choice for new installations.

Is a 5 Ton Trane system suitable for most Southern California homes?

A properly sized 5-ton unit works well for larger single-family homes with good insulation and typical cooling loads in the region.
