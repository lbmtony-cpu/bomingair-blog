Two 4-Ton AC PRO Heat Pump Systems Installation for Office

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Diamond Bar, CA

Project Overview

BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating recently completed the installation of two 4-ton AC PRO Heat Pump systems for office use at a business in the Southern California area. The existing equipment had reached the end of its reliable service life, leading to inconsistent temperatures and higher operating costs during peak seasons.

Why These Systems Were Chosen

AC PRO heat pumps offer strong heating and cooling performance in a single unit, making them well suited for commercial spaces that need year-round comfort without separate furnaces. The 4-ton capacity matched the calculated load for each zone, providing efficient operation while meeting the demands of daily office activity. This approach reduces equipment redundancy and simplifies maintenance.

Installation Process

Technicians removed the old units, prepared the roof and ground pads, and positioned the new condensers and air handlers. New line sets, thermostats, and condensate drains were installed to current code standards. Electrical connections and refrigerant charging were completed with precise measurements to ensure optimal efficiency from day one. The entire project was finished with minimal disruption to normal business hours.

Results and Customer Benefits

The office now maintains steady temperatures across all workspaces with quieter operation and improved humidity control. The dual heat pump setup gives the business reliable backup if one unit needs service. Owners can expect lower utility costs over time due to the equipment's efficiency ratings and proper sizing.
