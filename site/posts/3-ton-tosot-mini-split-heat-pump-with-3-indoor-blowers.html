3 Ton TOSOT Mini-Split Heat Pump with 3 Indoor Blowers

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Southern California

Project Overview

A homeowner in the Southern California area contacted BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating to upgrade an aging central system that struggled with uneven temperatures across multiple rooms. The solution selected was a 3 Ton Mini-Split Heat Pump, with 3 indoor blowers. Brand: TOSOT. This ductless configuration allowed precise control in three separate zones without extensive ductwork modifications.

Why This Equipment Choice

The TOSOT 3-ton outdoor unit paired with three indoor wall-mounted blowers provides reliable heating and cooling for homes up to approximately 1,500 square feet. Inverter technology adjusts compressor speed to match demand, reducing energy use compared with older single-stage systems. The multi-zone design eliminates hot and cold spots while offering independent temperature settings in each room.

Installation Process

Technicians first evaluated existing electrical service and refrigerant line routing. They mounted the outdoor condenser on a level pad, ran insulated line sets through an exterior wall, and positioned the three indoor units high on interior walls for optimal airflow. Electrical connections were completed, the system was pressure-tested, and a vacuum was pulled before charging with refrigerant. Final steps included condensate drainage verification and homeowner walkthrough.

Results and Homeowner Benefits

The new system delivers quiet, efficient operation year-round. Each indoor blower can be controlled separately, improving comfort while lowering utility costs. With fewer moving parts than a traditional forced-air setup, maintenance needs are reduced. Homeowners gain consistent temperatures, improved humidity control, and a cleaner aesthetic without bulky ducts.
