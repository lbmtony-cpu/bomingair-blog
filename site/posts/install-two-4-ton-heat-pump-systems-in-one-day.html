Install Two 4-Ton Heat Pump Systems in One Day

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Anaheim Hills, CA

Project Overview

BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating recently completed the install of two 4-ton heat pump systems in one day at a home in the Southern California area. The existing equipment had reached the end of its useful life, leading to inconsistent temperatures and higher energy use. The homeowners needed a solution that could restore reliable comfort quickly without extended disruption.

Why Two 4-Ton Heat Pump Systems

Each 4-ton unit was selected to match the cooling and heating load of separate zones within the home. Heat pumps provide both heating and cooling from a single system, which reduces the need for separate furnaces and improves overall efficiency. This approach is well suited to the mild winters and warm summers common here in the Chino Hills area.

The Installation Process

The crew arrived early and coordinated the removal of the old equipment before setting the new outdoor units and indoor air handlers. All refrigerant lines, electrical connections, and duct transitions were completed the same day. Final testing confirmed proper airflow and thermostat communication across both systems before the team left the property.

Benefits Delivered

The homeowners now enjoy steady temperatures throughout the house and lower monthly energy use. Because the work was finished in a single day, there was minimal impact on daily routines.
