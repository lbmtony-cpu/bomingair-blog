3-Ton Trane Heat Pump Installation

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Diamond Bar, CA

Project Overview

BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating recently completed an Install 3-Ton Heat Pump . Trane. at a home in the Southern California area. The existing system was at the end of its service life and no longer maintained consistent temperatures. The homeowner selected a new 3-ton Trane heat pump to restore reliable heating and cooling.

Why Choose a 3-Ton Trane Heat Pump

A 3-ton unit is properly sized for many single-story homes in our region. Trane equipment is known for durable construction and strong performance in both heating and cooling modes. The heat pump provides efficient operation year-round, reducing the need for separate heating equipment and helping homeowners manage energy use in Southern California’s variable climate.

Installation Process

Our technicians removed the old outdoor and indoor equipment, then installed the new Trane outdoor unit on a level pad with proper clearances. Inside, a matching air handler was set in place and connected to existing ductwork. All refrigerant lines were pressure-tested, the system was evacuated, and new electrical connections were made to meet code. Final steps included charging the system to factory specifications and verifying airflow and thermostat operation.

Results and Homeowner Benefits

The new 3-ton Trane heat pump now delivers steady temperatures throughout the house. The homeowner gains quieter operation, improved humidity control in summer, and dependable heat during cooler months. Routine maintenance will keep the system running efficiently for years.
