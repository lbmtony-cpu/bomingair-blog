3 Ton Goodman AC System Installation with R32 Refrigerant

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Southern California

Project Overview

A homeowner in the Southern California area needed to replace an aging air conditioning unit. BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating completed the installation of a 3 ton Goodman AC system with new refrigerant R32. The project focused on restoring reliable cooling while upgrading to a more efficient refrigerant.

Why This Equipment Choice

The 3 ton Goodman AC system paired with R32 refrigerant offers strong performance for typical residential loads in the region. R32 provides better heat transfer than older refrigerants and carries a lower global warming potential. Goodman equipment is known for straightforward serviceability and durable components, which helps reduce long-term maintenance needs.

Installation Process

The team removed the existing system, recovered the old refrigerant according to EPA guidelines, and prepared the lineset and electrical connections. They positioned the new outdoor condenser and indoor coil, pressure-tested the system, and charged it with R32. Final steps included airflow verification and thermostat setup to ensure balanced operation.

Results and Homeowner Benefits

The new installation delivers consistent temperatures and quieter operation. Improved efficiency from the R32 refrigerant helps lower energy use during peak summer months. Homeowners gain peace of mind from a manufacturer-backed system that is easier to service in the future.
