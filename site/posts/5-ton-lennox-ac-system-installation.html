5 Ton Lennox AC System Installation in Southern California

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Southern California

Project Overview

BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating recently completed the installation of a 5 ton Lennox AC system at a home in the Southern California area. The existing unit had reached the end of its reliable service life, leading to inconsistent cooling during peak summer months. After a thorough on-site assessment, the team recommended replacing it with a new 5 ton Lennox model to match the home's cooling load requirements.

Why a 5 Ton Lennox AC System

Lennox equipment is recognized for its strong build quality and performance in demanding climates. A properly sized 5 ton unit provides balanced cooling capacity without excessive cycling, which helps maintain steady indoor temperatures. Homeowners in the Chino Hills area often face high daytime heat, so selecting equipment known for durability supports longer service intervals and consistent operation.

Installation Process

The project began with safe removal of the old system, followed by inspection of the existing ductwork and electrical connections. New refrigerant lines were installed with proper insulation, and the outdoor condenser was positioned on a level pad for optimal airflow. The indoor air handler was secured and connected, after which the team performed a full system evacuation, charging, and operational testing to verify correct pressures and thermostat communication.

Results and Homeowner Benefits

The new 5 ton Lennox AC system delivers even cooling throughout the property while operating more quietly than the previous unit. Improved energy performance helps reduce monthly utility costs over time, and the equipment's warranty coverage adds peace of mind. Routine maintenance will keep the system running efficiently for years. For professional HVAC services, contact BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating.
