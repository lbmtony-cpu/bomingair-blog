2-Ton LENNOX Heatpump Roof Unit Installation

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Southern California

Project Overview

A 2-Ton LENNOX Heatpump Roof Unit was installed on a commercial building in the Southern California area. The existing rooftop system had reached the end of its reliable service life and was no longer maintaining consistent temperatures.

Why a 2-Ton LENNOX Heatpump Roof Unit

This model offers a compact footprint suited for roof-mounted applications while delivering both heating and cooling from a single unit. Lennox equipment is known for durable compressors and corrosion-resistant coils, which perform well in the region's varying weather conditions.

Installation Process

The team removed the old unit, inspected the existing curb and duct connections, then set the new 2-Ton LENNOX Heatpump Roof Unit in place. Electrical and control wiring were updated to current code, refrigerant lines were pressure-tested, and the system was commissioned for proper airflow and thermostat communication.

Customer Gains

The property now receives reliable year-round comfort with fewer service calls. The new unit improves energy efficiency compared with the previous aging equipment and reduces the risk of unexpected downtime during peak seasons. Owners benefit from quieter operation and straightforward maintenance access on the roof.
