1 Ton GREE Mini-Split Heat Pump Installation

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Montclair, CA

Project Overview

A homeowner in the Chino Hills area needed reliable year-round comfort for a smaller space without the expense or disruption of a full ducted system. BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating recommended and installed a 1 ton Mini-Split Heat Pump system, Brand GREE. This equipment provides both cooling and heating in a compact, efficient package suited to Southern California’s mild winters and warm summers.

Why a GREE 1 Ton Mini-Split Heat Pump

The 1 ton Mini-Split Heat Pump system, Brand GREE offers inverter-driven technology that adjusts output to match demand, reducing energy waste. Without ductwork, it avoids the efficiency losses common in older central systems. Homeowners gain quiet operation, precise temperature control in the served room, and strong performance even when outdoor temperatures drop.

Installation Process

Technicians first assessed the space and selected an optimal indoor unit location for even airflow. The outdoor condenser was positioned on a level pad with proper clearances. Line sets were routed neatly through an exterior wall, vacuumed, and leak-tested before charging. Electrical connections were completed to code, and the system was commissioned with performance verification. The entire process was completed in one day with minimal impact on daily routines.

Customer Gains and Long-Term Value

After startup the homeowner immediately noticed consistent temperatures and lower noise compared with window units. The system’s high efficiency helps manage monthly energy use while delivering reliable comfort. Regular maintenance from BOMING will keep the 1 ton Mini-Split Heat Pump system, Brand GREE performing at peak levels for years.
