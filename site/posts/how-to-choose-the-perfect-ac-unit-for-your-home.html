How to Choose the Perfect AC Unit for Your Home

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Southern California

Evaluating the Home's Cooling Requirements

A homeowner in the Southern California area contacted BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating to address inconsistent cooling and rising energy costs. The existing system was aging and no longer matched the home's square footage or insulation levels. Our technicians performed a detailed load calculation, considering window placement, ductwork condition, and local climate demands before recommending options.

Selecting the Right Equipment

Choosing the perfect AC unit for your home requires matching capacity, efficiency rating, and features to the property. We presented several properly sized models with high SEER ratings suitable for Chino Hills area summers. The selected unit offered variable-speed technology for better humidity control and quieter operation while meeting the home's exact cooling load.

Installation Steps and Considerations

The installation began with safe removal of the old equipment. New refrigerant lines were run, the evaporator coil was matched to the condenser, and duct connections were sealed to prevent air loss. Electrical upgrades ensured compatibility, and the system was charged and tested for optimal performance. All work followed local codes and manufacturer specifications.

Results and Long-Term Value

The homeowner now enjoys steady temperatures throughout the house and lower monthly utility bills. Proper sizing and professional installation prevent short cycling and extend equipment life. Regular maintenance from our team will keep the system running reliably for years.
