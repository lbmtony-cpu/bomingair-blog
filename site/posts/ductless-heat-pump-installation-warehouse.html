Ductless Heat Pump Installation for Warehouse

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Chino, CA

Project Overview

A warehouse business in the Southern California area needed better temperature control for its large open space. The existing system was inconsistent, leading to hot and cold spots that affected both workers and stored materials. BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating was called to perform an install ductless heat pump for warehouse use.

Why a Ductless Heat Pump Was Selected

Ductless systems avoid the need for extensive ductwork, which is often impractical in warehouse settings. These units provide zoned heating and cooling, allowing different areas to maintain comfortable temperatures independently. They also offer strong energy efficiency, helping reduce operating costs while delivering reliable performance year-round in the region's variable climate.

Installation Process

The team assessed the layout to determine optimal indoor unit placement for even air distribution. Outdoor condensers were positioned for proper airflow and minimal disruption. Lines were run discreetly through walls and ceilings, and the system was commissioned to ensure quiet, efficient operation. The entire process was completed with limited impact on daily warehouse activities.

Results and Benefits

The new system now provides consistent comfort across the space, improving working conditions and protecting inventory from temperature swings. Lower energy use and reduced maintenance needs give the business greater reliability. Owners appreciate the clean installation and long-term performance without major structural changes.
