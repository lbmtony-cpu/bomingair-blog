Installation of 5-Ton BOSCH Heat Pump System

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Diamond Bar, CA

Project Overview

BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating recently completed an installation of 5-ton BOSCH heat pump system at a home in the Southern California area. The project replaced an aging HVAC setup with a modern, all-electric heat pump solution sized for the property's cooling and heating demands.

Why a 5-Ton BOSCH Heat Pump

BOSCH heat pumps are recognized for their strong efficiency ratings and reliable performance in mild climates like ours. A 5-ton unit provides balanced capacity for larger homes without excessive cycling. This approach supports both cooling in summer and heating in winter from a single system, reducing the need for separate equipment.

The Installation Process

Technicians began by safely removing the old equipment and preparing the site. The new outdoor BOSCH condenser was positioned on a level pad with proper clearances, while the indoor air handler was installed and connected to existing ductwork. Electrical upgrades ensured compatibility, followed by refrigerant charging, airflow balancing, and full system testing. The entire process was completed with attention to local codes and manufacturer guidelines.

Results and Homeowner Benefits

The homeowner now enjoys steady temperatures and quieter operation. The heat pump's variable-speed technology helps maintain comfort while supporting lower energy use compared with older systems.
