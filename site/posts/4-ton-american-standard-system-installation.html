4-Ton American Standard System Installation

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Upland, CA

Project Overview

BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating recently completed the installation of a 4-ton American Standard system at a home in the Southern California area. The existing equipment had reached the end of its reliable service life, prompting the homeowners to seek a modern, efficient replacement suited to the region's warm climate.

Why This Equipment Choice

The 4-ton American Standard system was selected for its balance of performance and durability. American Standard units are known for consistent cooling output and solid construction, making them suitable for homes across the Chino Hills area where summer temperatures can climb quickly. The size matched the home's cooling load without excess capacity that could lead to short cycling.

Installation Process

The project began with removal of the old outdoor condenser and indoor air handler. New refrigerant lines were run where needed, and the team ensured proper airflow through the ductwork. Electrical connections were updated to meet current codes, and the system was charged and tested for correct operation. The entire process was completed in one day with minimal disruption to the household.

Results and Homeowner Benefits

After startup, the home achieved steady indoor temperatures with lower humidity levels. The new system operates more quietly than the previous unit and is expected to deliver improved energy efficiency. Homeowners now have a reliable cooling solution backed by a manufacturer warranty and professional installation from a licensed local contractor.
