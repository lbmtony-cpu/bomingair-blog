3-Ton Heat Pump Installation: Best Gas Furnace Replacement

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Upland, CA

Understanding the Need for a Heat Pump Replacement

A homeowner in the Southern California area contacted BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating after their existing gas furnace began showing signs of inefficiency and inconsistent performance. The old system struggled to maintain steady temperatures during cooler months while offering no cooling capability. After a thorough assessment, our team recommended a 3-ton heat pump installation as the best replacement for gas furnace systems in this climate.

Why a 3-Ton Heat Pump Makes Sense Here

Heat pumps deliver both heating and cooling from a single unit, which suits the mild winters and warm summers common in the Chino Hills area. Unlike gas furnaces that only provide heat, this equipment reverses refrigerant flow to remove indoor heat during summer. It operates efficiently at lower energy use because it moves heat rather than generating it through combustion. Homeowners gain quieter operation, fewer moving parts, and no need for gas lines or combustion venting.

The Installation Process

Our technicians first safely removed the old gas furnace and prepared the existing ductwork. The new 3-ton heat pump required an outdoor condenser unit, an indoor air handler, and updated electrical connections. We ensured proper refrigerant line sizing, drainage, and thermostat integration. The entire process was completed with minimal disruption, restoring full heating and cooling within one day.

Benefits Experienced After Installation

The new system provides consistent temperatures throughout the home without the dry air or hot spots common with gas heat. Because heat pumps run on electricity, the household eliminated gas usage for heating. Regular maintenance keeps the equipment reliable for many seasons. Overall, the homeowner now enjoys improved comfort and lower utility costs during both heating and cooling periods.

If your gas furnace is aging, contact BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating to discuss whether a 3-ton heat pump installation is the best replacement for gas furnace needs in your home.
