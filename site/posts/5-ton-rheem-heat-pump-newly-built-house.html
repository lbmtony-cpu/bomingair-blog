5-Ton Rheem Heat Pump for Newly-Built House

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Yorba Linda, CA

Project Overview

BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating recently completed installation of a 5-ton heat pump Rheem at a newly-built house in the Southern California area. The system was selected to meet the home’s full heating and cooling needs from day one of occupancy.

Why a 5-Ton Rheem Heat Pump

A 5-ton heat pump Rheem offers reliable performance in our local climate while delivering both heating and cooling from a single unit. Rheem equipment is known for durable compressors and efficient operation, which helps keep energy use reasonable in new construction that often features better insulation and tighter envelopes.

Installation Process

Technicians coordinated with the builder during final construction stages. The outdoor unit was placed on a level pad with proper clearances, while the indoor air handler was mounted in the designated mechanical space. New line sets were run, the system was pressure-tested, and all electrical and thermostat connections were completed to manufacturer specifications. Final startup and testing confirmed correct airflow and refrigerant charge.

Results and Homeowner Benefits

The homeowner now has consistent temperatures throughout the house and quiet operation. Because the equipment was installed during construction, ductwork and electrical planning were optimized, reducing future service needs. Regular maintenance will help the system deliver dependable comfort for years.
