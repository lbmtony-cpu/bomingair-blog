3 Ton Goodman AC System Installation with R32 Refrigerant

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Southern California

Project Overview

BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating recently completed the installation of a 3 ton Goodman AC system with new refrigerant R32 at a home in the Southern California area. The existing older unit had reached the end of its reliable service life and was no longer cooling efficiently.

Why Choose a 3 Ton Goodman AC System with R32

The Goodman brand is known for dependable performance and straightforward maintenance. Pairing it with R32 refrigerant offers improved energy efficiency compared to older refrigerants while meeting current environmental standards. This combination provides steady cooling capacity suited to many homes in the Chino Hills area without unnecessary complexity.

Installation Process

Our technicians removed the old equipment, verified proper sizing for the home's cooling load, and installed the new 3 ton outdoor condenser along with a matching indoor air handler. New line sets were run where needed, the system was pressure tested, and it was charged with R32 refrigerant. Final steps included electrical connections, thermostat integration, and a full operational test.

Results for the Homeowner

The new system delivers consistent temperatures and quieter operation. Homeowners gain better humidity control during warm months and reduced risk of unexpected breakdowns.
