Four AC Systems Installed in Two Days: Three Gas, One Heat Pump

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Southern California

Project Overview

A property in the Southern California area needed reliable cooling and heating across multiple zones. BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating completed the installation of four AC systems in two days, consisting of three gas systems and one heat pump. The project focused on minimizing downtime while delivering consistent performance for the entire home.

Equipment Choices

Three gas AC systems were selected for their strong heating capability during cooler months common in the Chino Hills area. The heat pump offers efficient cooling and supplemental heating, making it suitable for milder periods. This combination provides balanced operation without over-relying on a single technology. The approach matches the property’s layout and usage patterns for steady comfort year-round.

Installation Process

Work began with careful removal of older equipment and preparation of existing ductwork. Crews coordinated the placement of all four units efficiently, completing electrical, refrigerant, and gas connections across both days. Testing followed each installation to verify airflow, thermostat communication, and overall system function. The two-day timeline kept disruption low while maintaining quality standards.

Results and Homeowner Benefits

Residents now experience even temperatures and improved humidity control throughout the property. The updated systems support better energy use and require less frequent service compared with older equipment. Reliability increases with modern components designed for Southern California conditions. Homeowners gain peace of mind from professional installation and equipment suited to their needs.
