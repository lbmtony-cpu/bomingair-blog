1.5 Ton Daikin Mini-split System Installation

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Southern California

Project Overview

BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating recently completed installation of a 1.5 Ton Daikin Mini-split System at a residence in the Southern California area. The homeowner needed reliable zoned cooling without the expense or disruption of a full central system.

Why a 1.5 Ton Daikin Mini-split System

Daikin equipment is recognized for consistent performance and energy efficiency. A 1.5-ton capacity suits smaller spaces or individual rooms that require independent temperature control. The system operates quietly, uses inverter technology to adjust output, and avoids duct losses common in traditional setups.

Installation Process

Technicians began with a site assessment to confirm load requirements and optimal unit placement. The outdoor condenser was positioned on a level pad with proper clearances. Inside, the wall-mounted evaporator was mounted high on an exterior wall. Line sets were run through a small penetration, insulated, and connected. Electrical work included a dedicated circuit and condensate drainage. Final steps covered system evacuation, charging, and testing of all modes.

Benefits Delivered

The homeowner now enjoys precise temperature control in the targeted area along with lower operating costs compared with older window units. Maintenance access is straightforward and the equipment carries a strong manufacturer warranty when serviced regularly. For properties in the Chino Hills area seeking efficient spot cooling, this approach provides comfort without over-sizing.
