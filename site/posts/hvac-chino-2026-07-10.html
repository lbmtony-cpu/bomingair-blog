Chino HVAC Leak Detection and Sealing July 2026

By BOMING Air · July 13, 2026 · Serving Chino, CA

Thermal Imaging of Ceiling Vent

Technicians began by using a thermal camera to examine the ceiling air vent. This step identifies temperature variations that may indicate airflow or insulation issues common in Chino homes during peak summer heat.

Refrigerant Pressure Diagnostics

A Fieldpiece digital manifold gauge was connected to measure system pressures. Accurate readings help confirm whether refrigerant levels support efficient operation in Southern California’s high-temperature conditions.

Application of EasySeal LS/UV

The team introduced EasySeal LS/UV sealant near the AC unit. This product targets small leaks that can develop in older linesets exposed to local weather cycles.

Outdoor Unit Inspection

The outdoor HVAC unit was examined, including exposed wiring and the warning label. Visual checks ensure electrical connections remain secure and compliant with safety standards.

These steps matter for Chino and SoCal homeowners because summer temperatures often exceed 95°F, placing continuous demand on cooling equipment. Detecting and addressing minor leaks early helps maintain consistent performance and reduces unnecessary energy use.
