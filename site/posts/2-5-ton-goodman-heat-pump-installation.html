2.5 Ton Goodman Heat Pump Installation

By BOMING Air · July 1, 2026 · Serving Southern California

Project Overview

BOMING Air Conditioning & Heating recently completed the installation of 2.5 ton Goodman Heat pump system at a home in the Southern California area. The existing equipment had reached the end of its reliable service life, prompting the homeowners to seek a modern, efficient replacement.

Why a 2.5 Ton Goodman Heat Pump System

A 2.5-ton capacity matched the home's cooling and heating load calculations. Goodman systems are known for straightforward design, solid warranties, and good energy performance in mild Southern California climates. The heat pump provides both cooling in summer and heating in winter without requiring a separate furnace.

Installation Process

Technicians removed the old outdoor condenser and indoor air handler. New line sets were properly sized and insulated. The outdoor pad was leveled, the new Goodman condenser was set, and the indoor unit was mounted with correct condensate drainage. Electrical connections were updated to code, the system was pressure-tested, evacuated, and charged. Final startup confirmed proper airflow and temperature split.

Results and Homeowner Benefits

The new equipment delivers quieter operation and more consistent temperatures. Homeowners now have reliable year-round comfort with lower operating costs compared to the aging unit. Proper installation also supports the manufacturer's warranty coverage.
